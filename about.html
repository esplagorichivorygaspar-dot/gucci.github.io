About GUCCI

Italian Excellence Since 1921

Luxury Heritage

Founded in Florence in 1921 by Guccio Gucci, GUCCI has been synonymous with luxury, quality, and Italian craftsmanship for over a century.

Premium Quality

We use only the finest materials including premium leather, silk, and precious metals to create timeless pieces that last generations.

Iconic Design

Our bold and eclectic designs have become iconic symbols of luxury fashion recognized and celebrated worldwide.

Our Rich History

1921

Foundation

Guccio Gucci opens the first GUCCI shop in Florence, Italy, specializing in leather goods and luggage.

1940s

Innovation

Introduction of bamboo handle bags and signature red and green striped webbing during material shortages.

1960s

Global Expansion

GUCCI becomes a symbol of Italian sophistication and expands internationally with iconic designs.

Today

Modern Era

Continuing to set trends in luxury fashion while honoring our heritage and craftsmanship traditions.

Our Mission

To create exceptional luxury products that embody Italian craftsmanship, innovative design, and timeless elegance. We strive to inspire confidence and express individuality through our collections while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and ethical practices.
